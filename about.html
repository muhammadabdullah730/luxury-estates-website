About Luxury Estates

Karachi's premier luxury real estate agency

Our Story

Founded in 2008, Luxury Estates began as a boutique real estate agency specializing in high-end properties in Karachi's most exclusive neighborhoods. What started as a small team with a passion for architecture and design has grown into the city's most trusted name in luxury real estate.

Our founder, Sarah Johnson, recognized a need for a more personalized approach to luxury property transactions. While other agencies focused on volume, we dedicated ourselves to providing white-glove service to a select clientele of discerning buyers and sellers.

Over the past 15 years, we've helped over 1,200 clients find their dream homes or achieve exceptional returns on their real estate investments. Our portfolio now includes some of the most prestigious properties in Karachi, from historic mansions to cutting-edge modern villas.

Despite our growth, we maintain the same commitment to excellence, discretion, and personalized service that defined our early years. Each member of our team shares a deep knowledge of the local market and a genuine passion for connecting clients with properties that exceed their expectations.

Our Mission

At Luxury Estates, our mission is to redefine luxury real estate in Karachi through unparalleled service, market expertise, and a commitment to our clients' complete satisfaction.

We believe that buying or selling a high-end property should be an exceptional experience from start to finish. Our team goes above and beyond to ensure every transaction is seamless, stress-free, and tailored to our clients' unique needs.

Beyond transactions, we're building lasting relationships with our clients, becoming their trusted advisors for all their real estate needs throughout their lives.
